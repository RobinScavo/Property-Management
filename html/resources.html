Easy Online Application Process

Once you find a home you like, you will need to fill out an application (all residents are required to apply separately).

Our online application is fast, simple, and secure. Follow the steps below to apply for the property you are interested in.

If you are unable to apply online, please give us a call for additional options.

1.

Know the Rental Requirements

  • Before submitting an application, please watch the short video provided below & download and read MPM's rental criteria
  • As a company, we do business in accordance with the Federal Fair Housing Law and welcome Persons of all Race, Color, Religion, Sex, Handicap, Familial Status, or National Origin.

2.

Check for Pending Applications

  • When we receive multiple applications on a property, tenants are selected on a "most qualified" basis.
  • You may call us to ask if there are any pending applications for the property you are interested in prior to applying.

3.

Submit Your Application

  • Before submitting ANY Co-Applicant or Co-Signer Apps, your Primary Applicant MUST start their app and INVITE you to begin your application.
  • Do NOT use AUTO FILL while filling out your application.
  • There is a one-time, $50 non-refundable application fee for all applicants and co-signers. If your application is not approved for any reason, no refunds are given.
  • If your application is approved, but not selected for your first choice property, your application may be eligible for other available properties, without paying an additional application fee.
  • The Apply Online button accesses our secure online application portal.

Must Love Pets Policy

People with furry family members will find many pet friendly rental properties here. Additional deposits and monthly pet fees apply to all pet approved properties, and are clearly marked throughout the website. If our staff is helping you search, please let them know about your pet so they can recommend the best rentals.

We have partnered with a Pet Screening company to help manage our tenant's pets and to ensure adherence to our pet policies. Please click here to learn more about pet screening and to submit your pet application. There is a non-refundable $20 application screening fee for the first pet, $15 for the second pet and $0 for assistance/service animals.

Please also download and read our Pet Criteria so you understand all requirements before applying for a property.

Maintenance

Unfortunately, things do need repaired from time-to-time and your home has been furnished with appliances and utilities that must be kept in working order.

Our job is to make sure those items get repaired as quickly as possible during our business hours.

Your job is to follow the guidelines for handling and reporting emergency issues and non-emergency repairs. If you are not in danger, please troubleshoot your issue first before reporting an emergency or submitting a non-emergency repair request.

Emergency Repair Issues

Emergency issues that must be reported immediately are:

  • Gushing water
  • Lack of heat
  • Electrical shorts
  • Only toilet is not functioning
  • Inoperable door lock

Non-Emergency Repairs

Only use the Submit Repair Request form AFTER you have made every effort to and have concluded you are in need of a non-emergency repair.
